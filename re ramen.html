Re Ramen — restaurant website and online menu

Re Ramen is a cozy ramen bar serving handcrafted broths and seasonal bowls. The site focuses on a fast, mobile‑first browsing experience that highlights the signature menu, weekly specials, and inviting ambience. Key sections include a visual gallery, structured menu categories (starters, ramen, sides, drinks), branch location with opening hours, and clear calls to action for reservations and phone/WhatsApp inquiries. The build is bilingual (EN/AR), fully responsive, and optimized for accessibility and performance.
